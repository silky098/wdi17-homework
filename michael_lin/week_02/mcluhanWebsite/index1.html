How did a book smart, avuncular, Canadian professor tap into the zeitgeist of the American 1960's?


The global village, creates a new world, a totally new psychic outlook was originally coined by Marshall McLuhan when he philosophized the impact of television as an American mainstay.


He would later go on to predict the birth of the internet, 35 years before its existence. More over, he would go on to write about the impacts of mass content distribution for other mediums, such as print media, and television. He is also credited for coining the word "surfing" to describe how we move through media and information.
