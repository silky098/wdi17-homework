Where She Lived

Visions in the Lake

Being born in the hyper city that was New York, a person could either sink or swim. Our "Amazing Grace" not only floated, but virtually walked on water!

As a child she might have stood at the edge of the lake in Central Park and peered into its water to glimpse the magnitude of the seven seas. Then, seeing the pixelated reflection of the surrounding skyscrapers, pondered the complexity of the cyber cities to come. It is no big surprise that she grew up to be a naval offficer of the highest rank and a computer science genius of impeccable repute.
