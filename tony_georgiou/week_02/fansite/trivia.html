Why We Will Always Admire Her:

Because she was the REAL deal!

  1. * At the tender age of seven she dismantled seven clocks trying to find out how their alarms worked.

  2. * Applied for early entry to Vassar College to study Mathemtaics and Physics at the age of 16. She was refused entry, but was admitted the following year. She later continued her studies at Yale.

  3. * At Harvard she worked on one of the very first mainframe computers ever built. It was 51 feet long, 8 feet high and 8 feet long. Now the same amount of computer memory would take up a space smaller than pin head.
    4. ,
  4. * In 1959 she was part of a committee that defined the then new computer language, COBOL (Common Business-Oriented Language).

  5. * She coined the phrase "debugging" after physically removing a moth that had got stuck in the university. That moth is still preserved at the Smithsonian Institute!

  6. * She joined the US Navy at the age of 37 to help in the fight against Nazism.

  7. * Grace spent over 43 years in the navy. She retired at the age of sixty, but was called back into service.

  8. * She left the navy with the rank of Rear Admiral Lower (formerly known as Commodore). Thatis just two ranks below a full admiral.

  9. * There is a US navy ship named in her honour, the USS Hopper.

  10. * She is still known as the "Queen of Software."
